They were near Camp David at the time, and their hatred of President Mosswell being in his second term made him a primary target. The terrorist was able to breach the perimeter unharmed, unable to be stopped by the agents affected by the apathy field, despite the fact they were aware of the horror happening. For some reason or another, Aziel too was affected by the apathy field, but he was able to resist the anomaly’s effects. Just before the unidentified terrorist wielding SCP-668 made it to the Lodge’s door, Aziel was able to resist the anomaly's effects enough to put two shots in the terrorist, causing him to drop the knife and cease the effects.
